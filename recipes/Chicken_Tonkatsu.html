Chicken Tonkatsu

A recipe for delicious Japanese fried chicken and an accompanying Tonkatsu sauce. Serve with your choice of shredded cabbage, rice, or even mashed potatoes.

Ingredients

    sauce

  • ½ cup Worcestershire sauce
  • ¼ cup ketchup
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • pepper to taste

    • Chicken

  • 2 cups vegetable oil, for deep-fat frying
  • ½ cup all-purpose flour
  • ½ cup panko bread crumbs
  • salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 egg, beaten
  • 2 skinless, boneless chicken breast halves - pounded to 1/4 inch thickness
  • 1 green onion, thinly sliced

Directions

  1. step 1

    2. For the sauce, stir together the Worcestershire sauce, ketchup, and soy sauce, and a pinch of pepper to taste. Set aside.

  2. step 2

    3. Heat oil in deep-fryer to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
